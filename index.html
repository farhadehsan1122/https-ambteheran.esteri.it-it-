List and details on the main services provided by Italian Consular Offices, institutional website of the MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS.

Consular services are provided according to the principles of equality, impartiality, efficiency and transparency.

The Consulates ensure all compatriots protection in case of violation of their fundamental rights and limitation or deprivation of their personal freedom. They also provide assistance in emergency cases, for the search for family members, for succession procedures opened abroad and for the repatriation of remains.

Self-certifications and online certificates
 We hereby inform our fellow citizens that following the entry into force of the stability law (Art. 15 L. 183/2011) public offices are prohibited from issuing certificates to be shown to other public administrations and managers of public services, (Art. 40 DPR 445/2000).
Certifications issued by public administrations regarding statuses, personal qualities and facts are valid and usable only in relations between private individuals.
Therefore, if the requested certificate is needed to be presented to another public administration, a self-certification declaration form can be filled out (…). If, however, it is needed for a private entity, citizens registered in the Italian registry offices (APR and AIRE) can obtain most registry certificates directly and free of charge online. For other cases, please send your request to consolato.teheran@esteri.it

Anagraphic Certificates issued by Italian Municipalities

From November 15, 2021, citizens registered in the Italian registry offices (APR and AIRE) will be able to request the registry certificates listed below directly online, for themselves or for family members registered with them.

The issuing of these certificates, which will be free of charge, requires the possession of a digital identity (Electronic Identity Card, Spid or National Services Card – CSN).

To obtain the certificates, the citizen must connect to the site:  https://www.anagrafenazionale.interno.it/servizi-al-cittadino/  and log in with one of the aforementioned digital identities.
